. The game is about a main character called SIte Admin. This Site Admin is trying to complete a "safety walk" for work. He is doing the safety walk at an industrial heavy lift yard. It is a marine heavy lift yard. The main character has to take pictures of different deficiencies he finds. He also has to avoid "safety guys" who are trying to stop him and get him out of the yard. The game is won by completing the safety walk and taking enough pictures in a specific time period without being caught by the "safety guys" The player loses if he is touched three times by the safety guys.
